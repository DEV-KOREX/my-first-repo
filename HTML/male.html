The human reproductive system

The Male Repoductive System

The table below shows the male reproductive structures and their functions.

Organ Function
Testes Produces sperm
Scrotum Place of testes
Penis Canal sperms from testes to vagina
Sperm tube(Vas deferens) Canal sperms from testes to the urethra
Seminal gland Produces fluid enabling sperms to move
Epididimis Place of maturity of sperm
Prostate gland Produce liquid to flow to sperm tube
