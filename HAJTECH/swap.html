STEP 1


Visit any of our offline stores for a physical valuation of your old device.

STEP 2

After physical inspection is completed, an amount is offered by for your old device. This serves as monetary credit towards the purchase of a new Mobile Device.

STEP 3

If price offered for your old device is acceptable to you, this will be deducted from the price of a brand new device of your choice. For instance, If old device is priced at N50,000 and new device is N100,000, you will be required to pay just N50,000 as the N50,000 from your old device will be deducted from the amount of the new device.
